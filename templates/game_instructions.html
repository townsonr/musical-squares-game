Here's How to Play:


Make sure to use headphones or have your computer volume turned up.



When you hover over a box, it becomes shaded green



When you click on a box, it will turn dark green



Find the dark green box on the grid and click on it to score a point



Don't touch the red boxes! This will end the game



Click the alert to begin each round


Gain one point for every correct box you click!

To level up, click the correct green box in every round.

Each level requires more correct consecutive clicks than the last.
