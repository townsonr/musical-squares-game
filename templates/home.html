Projects and Research

Current Project:

Studying the effetcs of music on collaborative and competitive behavior with Professor Mohammad Ghassemi.


Brief Overview

The environment that people are placed in can influence them to act in specific ways, such as more collaboratively or competitively.

Music and noise are important components of one's environment, so listening to different types of music should make people react in different ways to the same stimuli.

If the specific qualities of music that evoke specific reactions can be identified, we may be able to use computers to help create new music following these guidelines.
