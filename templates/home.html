Projects & Research

I am currently working on a project studying the effects of music on human behavior with Professor Ghassemi in the Human Augmentation and Artificial Intelligence Lab (HAAIL). The properties of the environment that a person is placed in can significantly influence their behavior. Musical environments can differ by genre, tempo, instrumentation, volume, lyrical content, and so on. I created a web game to test players' concentration and motor skills under different musical environments and am using data collected from player interactions with the game to guide my research.
